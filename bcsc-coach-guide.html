Getting Started — Add to Your iPhone
🔗
Open your team's link in Safari
Use the URL for your team above. Must be Safari — Chrome won't allow adding to home screen on iPhone.
📤
Tap the Share button
The share icon is at the bottom of Safari — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
➕
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add. The BCSC icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app.
🟢
Wait for the green "Live" indicator
When you open the app, wait for the top-right corner to show Live with a green dot before entering any data. This confirms it's connected and syncing.
💡
Tip: If it shows "Syncing…" for more than 10 seconds, close the app fully and reopen it. If you see "Tap to retry" — tap it and it will reconnect automatically.
2
The Four Tabs
🏆
Leaderboard
Live Best & Fairest standings, sorted automatically by points. Shows season record (W/L/D/GF/GA/GD) at the top. Updates instantly as votes are entered.
📅
Fixture
Full 18-round schedule. Shows season stats at the top. Tap any game to enter the result and score. Completed games show green on the left.
🗳️
Votes
Enter votes after each game. Select the round, mark attendance, assign 1st–5th, tally goals, then hit Save. Both coaches can enter votes — last save wins.
👕
Squad
Full player list with current Best & Fairest points and goals tally for the season.
3
How to Enter Votes After a Game
🗳️
Go to the Votes tab
Tap the Votes icon in the bottom nav bar.
🔢
Select the round
Tap the correct round pill at the top (e.g. R4). Rounds that already have votes saved show with a green border.
✅
Mark attendance
For each player, toggle the Played switch on if they were at the game. This counts their games played total.
🥇
Assign votes 1st through 5th
Tap the vote button next to each player. Each placement can only be given to one player — the app will warn you if you try to double up.
⚽
Add goals
Use the − / + buttons to record how many goals each player scored.
💾
Tap Save Votes
Hit the red Save Votes button at the bottom. You'll see a green "Votes saved ✓" confirmation. The leaderboard updates instantly.

4
How to Enter a Match Result
📅
Go to the Fixture tab
Tap the Fixture icon in the bottom nav bar.
👆
Tap the game
Tap any game row to open the result entry screen. You can update a result at any time — tap again and change it.
🏅
Select Win, Draw or Loss
Tap WIN, DRAW, or LOSS — it highlights in green, gold, or red.
🔢
Enter the score and save
Enter BCSC Goals and Opposition goals, then tap Save Result. The fixture and season stats update immediately.
5
Good to Know
🔄
Both coaches see the same data
The app is live — any change one coach makes appears on the other's screen within seconds. No need to share files or screenshots.
📵
Each team's data is completely separate
The Kangaroos, Wallabies and U12B apps are fully independent. Votes and results from one team never appear in another.
🔁
If the app gets stuck
Close the tab completely (swipe up to close Safari), reopen it, and wait for the green Live dot. A simple refresh sometimes won't reconnect — a full close always will.
✏️
You can edit votes and results any time
Nothing is locked. Go back to any round in Votes or any game in Fixture and update it. The leaderboard and stats recalculate instantly.
